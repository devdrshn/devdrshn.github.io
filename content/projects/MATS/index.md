Motion Aided Traffic Signal (MATS) makes use of a motion detecting system to enhance the traditional traffic signals by keeping them off until there is any traffic. This would greatly help to minimize unnecessary power consumption caused by traffic signals, especially during late hours. 
If we look around our roads, we can notice that traffic signals are not present in every junctions, that is hazardous. The reason for its absence may be due to the place receiving minimal traffic. But by adding a Motion Aided Traffic Signal, we can make the signal to turn on only when necessary i.e. at times of heavy traffic. 

* **LOGIC** 

The system contains sensors which determine the traffic, they are kept on various parts of the junction so as to accurately determine the existence of heavy traffic in the region. Under traffic circumstances, the traffic signal is activated.


Once activated, the system will autonomously find solution to clear out traffic. This can be accomplished by implementing concrete software logic using Computer Vision, Machine learning principles. Developing a fully localized system will enable this system to be implemented in remote locations where network coverage / traditional traffic signal implementation is difficult.

A highlight of this project is that it is built entirely from very cheap and basic parts, making it economically feasible and very easy to maintain.
