## Event

**RoboBlitz** was a Robotics event organized by EE batch of NSSCE 25-29 in association with IE(I) Electrical Chapter NSSCE.  <br> 
The event's main motive was to guide freshers to the world of Robotics. RoboBlitz consisted of three events: <br> 
* 1. Debate Competition on Robotics and Automation and it's effects on human creativity and employment aspects. <br>
* 2. A technical talk session by an Industry Expert, Mr. V Vijay who is also an alumni of NSSCE introduced freshers to basic robotics components, the underlying mechanisms and the pathway for them to follow. <br> 
* 3. A technical workshop where the the participants were provided kits to assemble a Line Follower Robot. This is one of the most basic projects in Robotics. <br> 

Personally, I was was the Event coordinator. Through that experience I have been able to develop many valuable skills and got exposure to the processes that goes behind organizing an event. <br> 
The notable skills I was able to acquire are:  <br> 
1. Formal Document Writing.  <br> 
2. Student and Staff Relations.  <br> 
3. Planning and executing Ideas. <br> 

I will carry on these skill to effectively contribute to any team which I may be part of the future.
